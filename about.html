Gather 'round for a scary story...

Monster Things first opened its doors in 2005 headed by the owner Theodore Mcgee. Before opening up his own store Theo managed at a Halloween store where he learned a lot about the customers and what it takes to run a costume store. With this gained knowledge in the Halloween business, Theo decided to get the family together and open up a Halloween store in Nashville Tennessee.

Monster Things is a family-owned business. What you will find at Monster Things that you will not find anywhere else is genuine people who really care about the Halloween season and are committed to helping you find what you're looking for. We hope to see you soon!
